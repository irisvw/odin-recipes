Home-made Pesto

Ingredients

  • Basil, three cups
  • Cheese, parmesan preferred, 3/4 cup
  • Olive oil, 1/2 cup
  • Pine nuts, 1/4 cup
  • Fresh garlic, 4 cloves
  • Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Preparation

  1. Lightly toast the pine nuts.
  2. Combine the ingredients in a food processor or blender.
  3. Blend until smooth. Enjoy!
